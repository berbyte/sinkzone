macOS Configuration

  1. Open System Preferences from the Apple menu
  2. Click Network in the System Preferences window
  3. Select your active connection (Wi-Fi or Ethernet)
  4. Click Advanced in the bottom right
  5. Go to the DNS tab
  6. Click the + button and add 127.0.0.1
  7. Move it to the top of the DNS servers list
  8. Click OK and then Apply
